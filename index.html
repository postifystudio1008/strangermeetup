Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I come alone?

Absolutely! In fact, almost everyone comes alone. It's the best way to mingle, and our icebreakers are designed exactly for that.

Q. Is it safe and verified?

100%. We manually screen all registrations, host it in a well-lit premium cafe, and have a strict code of conduct. Any misbehavior means immediate removal.

Q. I'm an introvert. Will it be awkward?

Not at all! You don't have to carry the conversation. Our structured games and activities do the hard work of breaking the ice for you.

Q. What if I don't know anyone?

That's exactly the point! 90% of the people who come to our meetups show up solo. The whole event is built specifically for making new friends from scratch.

Q. What should I wear?

Whatever makes you feel confident and chill. Smart casual, sneakers, or a cute top—just bring good energy and a smile!
