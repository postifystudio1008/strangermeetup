Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I come alone?

Absolutely! The entire event is designed for people walking in solo. The board games are meant to instantly break the ice.

Q. What kind of games will we play?

Light, highly interactive, and extremely easy-to-learn games (think social deduction, drawing, or conversation-starters). No monopoly, we promise.

Q. Do I need to be a coffee drinker?

Not at all! The cafe offers tea, coolers, and great food. The "cafe vibe" is just about the cozy atmosphere.

Q. What should I wear?

Smart casual or whatever makes you feel confident and comfortable sitting around a table!
